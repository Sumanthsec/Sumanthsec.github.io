An Estate Like No Other

Nestled amidst rolling hills bathed in golden sunlight, Crimson Slopes is a meticulously cultivated estate where ideal terroir, seasoned vines, and unwavering dedication converge to create wines of exceptional character and depth. Here, passion and tradition unite to craft experiences that linger long after the last sip.

Terra Rossa Soil

Our vibrant iron-rich clay provides optimal drainage while retaining just enough moisture to sustain the vines. Its mineral complexity imparts concentration, complexity, and remarkable longevity to our wines.

Rolling Hills

The gentle slopes of Pilot Hill offer a mosaic of microclimates, providing ideal sun exposure and cooling breezes. This balance preserves the grapes’ acidity and delicate aromatics, ensuring elegant and balanced wines.

Matured Vines

Deep-rooted vines, some decades old, yield fewer clusters but each berry bursts with concentrated flavor. The resulting wines boast rich tannins, intense colors, and structural integrity that allows them to age gracefully.

Experience the Journey

From terra rossa soils to rolling hills and matured vines, every step of our journey is a testament to our dedication. Join us as we continue to explore and honor the land that gives life to our exceptional wines.

Meticulous Viticulture

We cultivate classic Bordeaux varietals—Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Cabernet Franc, Petit Verdot, and Malbec. Each vine is meticulously tended, hand-harvested at optimal ripeness, and sorted rigorously to ensure only the finest fruit reaches the cellar.
